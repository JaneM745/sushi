Welcome to our pet sitting service
We offer a second to none pet sitting service in Dublin and Leinster taking care of all your needs


Take the stress (and guilt!) out of going away.
No more temper tantrums on the way home from the kennels or cattery!

We offer a full compliment of services to give you full comfort that your pet is being looked after & cared for while you are away.

We offer much more than traditional kennels or a cattery.
Your pet can have the added benefit of staying in their home environment and need not be subject to upheavals.

We offer the following services:

  1. Walking - Long or short walks as required
  2. Feeding - We can feed up to twice daily and all dietary requirements catered for!
  3. Cleaning litter tray
  4. Play and cuddle - unlimited quantities!
  5. Overnight - We will stay with your pet overnight
  6. Full day and Overnight -We will stay with your pet overnight and throughout the day. Premium Service!

We also offer the following supplemental services:

  1. Watering plants
  2. Bins
  3. Welcome Home Packages - Bread, milk, tasty treats and a newspaper of your choice!
